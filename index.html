About Hannah

Hello! I'm Hannah, a passionate dog care professional with over 8 years of experience working with dogs of all breeds, sizes, and temperaments. My journey began when I adopted my first rescue dog, Max, who needed special care and training.

I hold certifications in professional dog grooming from the National Dog Groomers Association of America, and I'm a certified dog trainer through the Certification Council for Professional Dog Trainers (CCPDT). My approach is always positive, patient, and tailored to each dog's individual personality and needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions about my services.

I serve the Greater Sacramento area primarily in Vacaville, Fairfield, Dixon, Travis, Davis, and West Sacramento. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside a 10-mile radius.

For the safety of all dogs in my care, I strongly advise for keeping your pet current for:

  • Rabies vaccination
  • DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus)
  • Bordetella (kennel cough)

Puppies must have completed their initial vaccination series.

For grooming, I recommend booking at least 1 weeks in advance, especially during busy seasons (holidays, summer). Training sessions can typically be scheduled within 1-2 weeks. For dog sitting, especially around holidays, please book at least 2-3 weeks in advance as spots fill quickly and I aim to conduct a pre-visit for familiarization with first time dogs.

Yes! I offer a 10% discount for:

  • Military/veterans
  • Seniors (65+)
  • First responders
  • Multiple dogs from the same household
  • Pre-paid packages of 5+ grooming sessions or training lessons

I require 24 hours notice for grooming cancellations and 48 hours for dog sitting. Cancellations with less notice will be charged 50% of the service fee. For training sessions, 24 hours notice is required or the session will be charged in full. Of course, emergencies and illnesses are exceptions to this policy.
