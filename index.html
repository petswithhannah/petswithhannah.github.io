Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

I serve the greater Sacramento area including Vacaville, Fairfield, Dixon, Davis, and Sacramento, CA. Please contact me to confirm availability for your specific location.

What's your experience with puppies or senior pets?

I have extensive experience caring for senior pets, pets with medical conditions, and those requiring medication. My pet CPR and first aid certification ensures I can handle emergencies, and I'm comfortable administering medications as needed.

How does the grooming process work for a student groomer?

As a student groomer, I work under supervision from experienced professionals to ensure quality and safety. I take extra time with each pet to make the experience positive, and my rates reflect my training status. I specialize in gentle, stress-free grooming with a focus on pet comfort.

What's included in your pet sitting service?

My pet sitting includes feeding per your instructions, fresh water, medication administration if needed, playtime, walks for dogs, litter box cleaning for cats, and lots of love and attention. I also bring in mail, water plants, and provide daily updates with photos so you can see how your pet is doing.
